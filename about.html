About

Loic Johl is a Canadian artist whose prime goal in his art is to innovate and "bring listeners to another world". Primarily a bassist, who has been playing since he was a child, alongside teaching himself how to produce and mix his own music around a similar age, Loic has carefully crafted his sound over years, linking to his own self-development as a human and his technical proficiency. His music is almost entirely made with the bass, using creative ways to layer this instrument alongside luscious vocals and introspective lyrics, creating a unique experience for listeners.
