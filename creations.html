CREATIONS

MELANCHOLIA

April 2025

Melancholia is a deeply personal song of mine I wrote around March of 2025. As I talk about often, I’ve always struggled with my self-identity, perception, insecurity… I wanted to write a song that embodies and personifies this creature and how it relates to our difficult lives. As is commonplace in my world, the bass starts us off, diving straight into an intense groove with the introspective lyrics. You can watch a “Making of” of the song here.

ETERNALLY

January 2025
